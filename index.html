A little bit about me

The person behind the PM. Here are a few things that make me, me.

Mumbai to UIUC

I grew up in the city that never stops — Mumbai taught me resilience, ambition, and the art of finding beauty in chaos. Leaving home wasn't just a flight; it was choosing to bet on myself. Today, navigating grad school in Champaign, I carry Mumbai's hustle in every deck I build and every problem I try to solve.

14 Years of Bharatnatyam

Outside of work, I spent 14 years learning Bharatanatyam as a hobby, performing on stage and participating in competitions along the way. It taught me discipline, consistency, and the confidence to perform under pressure—qualities that continue to influence how I approach problem-solving and collaboration today.

Always Up for a Coffee Chat

If there's one thing I genuinely love, it's a good conversation over coffee. Whether you're a founder, a fellow PM, or a student trying to break into tech — I'm always looking to connect and network. Networking isn't transactional to me; it's how I learn, grow, and find my people.

Books I Keep Revisiting

Think Again, Atomic Habits, Inspired, The Lean Product Playbook — these aren't just books, they're mental models I return to whenever I'm stuck or building something new. I believe the best product thinking often starts outside a product context.
